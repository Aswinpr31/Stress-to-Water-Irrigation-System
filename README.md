## **Project Documentation**

### **For Software:**

1. **Overview**
   The *Stress-to-Water Irrigation System ‚Äì Funny Edition* is a browser-based farming simulation game where rapid clicks generate stress energy to power a motor, pump water, grow crops, eliminate pests, and earn coins‚Äîall with humorous animations and sound effects.

2. **Technologies Used**

   * **HTML5** ‚Äì Game layout & structure
   * **CSS3** ‚Äì Styling, animations, and responsive design
   * **JavaScript (ES6+)** ‚Äì Game logic & interactivity
   * **Web Audio API** ‚Äì Sound effects & background music
   * **LocalStorage** ‚Äì Save high scores & coins

3. **Game Flow**

   1. Player selects a crop type (Rice, Corn, Wheat)
   2. Click rapidly to generate stress energy ‚Üí motor spins faster
   3. Motor pumps water ‚Üí crops grow in stages
   4. Eliminate pests & collect power-ups
   5. Harvest mature crops ‚Üí earn points & coins
   6. Repeat to beat the high score

4. **Core Features**

   * **Stress-to-energy system**
   * **Animated motor & water flow**
   * **Crop growth cycle**
   * **Pest management**
   * **Power-ups & bonuses**
   * **Scoring & coin collection**

5. **System Requirements**

   * Any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
   * JavaScript enabled
   * Audio output for sound effects (optional)
